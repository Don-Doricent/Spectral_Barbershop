Our Services

Take a look at what we have to offer.

  • Haircut

    A basic haircut service that includes trimming and shaping of hair on the head and neckline. Pricing ranges from $20-$40 depending on the experience level of the barber.

  • Beard Trimming

    A service that involves trimming and shaping of facial hair, including beard, mustache, and sideburns. Pricing ranges from $15-$25.

  • Shave

    A traditional barber shop service that involves a straight razor shave, hot towel treatment, and facial massage. Pricing ranges from $30-$50.

  • Hair treatments

    A service that includes a variety of hair treatments, such as deep conditioning, keratin treatments, and scalp treatments. Pricing ranges from $25-$75..

  • Scalp Massage

    A relaxing massage of the scalp that helps to improve blood circulation, reduce stress, and promote hair growth. Pricing ranges from $15-$30.

  • Hair Styling

    A service that includes a variety of styling options for men's hair, including blowouts, fades, and styling with gel, wax, or pomade. Pricing ranges from $25-$50.
